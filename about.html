Hey y'all, I’m Jesse. I've always been obsessed with music.

Growing up in Waco, Texas, I played many instruments in church and school, and spent a lot of time learning Beatles songs. I took jazz guitar lessons for many years, and started my own rock band with my best friends. I attended the University of North Texas School of Music where I studied jazz and classical guitar, composition, and west African drumming.

Now based in Seattle, I’ve written and produced hundreds of original songs for myself and others.

My newest project, an album of original beats entitled “Our Fodder” will be out in early 2023.
